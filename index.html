Welcome to Beeek

Beeek is a powerful tool that converts your text or uploaded documents (TXT or PDF) into speech. Simply upload your file or type/paste your text, and Beeek will read it aloud for you. You can also rearrange and clean up your text using AI-powered tools. Choose from a variety of voices and adjust the speech speed to suit your preferences.
